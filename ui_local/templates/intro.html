Welcome to the Color Vision Survey

This survey is designed to understand how people perceive different colors. Your participation will help us to improve color accessibility and design choices in various applications.

Please click the start button below to begin the survey. Thank you for your participation and valuable contributions!

How to do the survey?

For each question, you will see a reference color on the left. Adjust the slider to modify the color on the right until you think the two colors match. Click the "Next" button the first time you perceive the colors to be the same (or with nuance difference). If you cannot find a matching point along the slider, click "No Match". If you see a matching interval, make sure to select the first occurrence.
